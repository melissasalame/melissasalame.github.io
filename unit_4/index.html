B*CURED's primary goal is to fund innovative brain cancer research to find a cure.

We invite clinical doctors and research scientists whose principal focus is brain cancer to apply for our grants. Clinical and translational projects with significant clinical promise will be considered.

Get Involved

Join Us

Brain cancer research is sorely underfunded. But you can help change that. Every donation helps B*CURED fund the vital research that will one day lead to the end of brain cancer.

Donations of every size are impactful. A donation in excess of $50,000 can fully fund a grant. Think about the power you have to change the course of this disease.

Click here to donate securely online now

or mail your tax-deductible donation to

  • B*CURED
  • PO Box 1071
  • Greenwich, CT 06836

B*CURED is a 501(c)(3) organization with Tax ID: 26-1906165

Matching Gifts: Your gift can be twice as generous thanks to a matching gift from your employer. Please check with your Human Resources Department to see if your company offers this powerful benefit.

Tribute Gifts: Consider honoring the journey of a loved one with brain cancer by designating B*CURED to receive donations in tribute or memory. You can create your own tribute page and ask others to join you. Click here to find out about our Tribute Program.

Community Service: You can create your own community service project to raise funds for B*CURED. Be creative to help save lives.

Here are some examples of tribute projects done for B*CURED:

  • A lemonade stand
  • A Sweet Sixteen donation in lieu of gifts
  • A Bat Mitzvah donation in lieu of gifts
  • Handmade bracelets
  • A 50 mile run

Other Resources

For more information about brain tumors and brain cancer, you may find the following sites useful:

  • ABC2 (Accelerate Brain Cancer Cure) ABC2 offers guidance including information on clinical trials, finding care, and other resources for brain cancer patients. www.abc2.org tel. 202-419-3140
  • American Brain Tumor Association ABTA offers in-depth facts and information about brain tumors. www.abta.org tel. 800-886-2282
  • National Brain Tumor Foundation This website offers a service to find support groups near you. www.braintumor.org tel. 800-934-2873
  • Brain Science Foundation: This website contains more information on meningiomas and primary brain tumors. www.brainsciencefoundation.org tel. 781-239-2903
  • To learn from other patients, please visit: www.smartpatients.com
